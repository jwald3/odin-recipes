Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  3. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop dough with an ice cream scoop and place on ungreased pan. Bake for about 10 minutes or until the cookies have golden brown edges
