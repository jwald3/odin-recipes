Chicago Pizza Bites

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 16 ounce package of cold, prepared pizza dough
  • 4 ounces provolone cheese, diced
  • 3/4 cup cooked, crumbled Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup cooked, diced sweet peppers
  • 4 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup prepared pizza sauce
  • 3/4 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Generously grease a standard muffin pan with 2 teaspoons of oil.
  2. Roll dough to a thickness of about 1/8 inch. Cut the dough into 4-inch circles, rerolling dough if necessary. (Tip: rolling dough onto a floured surface helps prevent stickiness.)
  3. Place each circle of dough into the oiled muffin cup neatly into the center with edges gently pressed to the sides of the cup
  4. Put a tablespoon of provolone cheese, followed by a tablespoon of cooked sausage and two teaspoons of cooked peppers. Lightly compress the toppings inward to free space for 1 tablespoon of mozzarella cheese and 2 tablespoons of pizza sauce. Press toppings one more time and sprinkle Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese on top.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, aiming for golden brown crust and deliciously melty toppings.
  6. Remove pan from the oven and provide 10-15 minutes for the pizzas to cool while in the tray. Remove each pizza by using a knife along the edge to separate each one from its cup and then using a fork to lift it out.
  7. Allow pizzas to cool on a rack for a few minutes before plating. (Tip: garnish pizzas with a dusting of parsley for added style!)
