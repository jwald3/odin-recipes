Baked Parmesan Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 8 (5 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Line a pan with foil.
  2. Put olive oil in a bowl. Mix dry ingredients together in another bowl
  3. Cut chicken thighs in half. Dip each piece in olive oil, allowing for excess oil to run off. Then press chicken into Parmesan mixture to achieve an even coating.
  4. Place chicken on pan and bake for 25 minutes until an internal temperature of at least 165 degrees F is reached.
